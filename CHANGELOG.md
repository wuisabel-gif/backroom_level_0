# progress log

a running record of what's gone into Level 0. newest first.

## latest

- **unknown wanderers.** pale human figures (built from the player model) now roam
  the rooms. walk up to one and press `E` and it's a gamble — a **survivor** who
  marks the correct answer on your next M.E.G. note, or a **Skin-Stealer** who was
  never human and attacks. one is guaranteed to greet you a few steps from spawn,
  and they pace around instead of standing frozen.
- **the monster wakes at ~15 seconds.** consistent grace period at the start so you
  can read a note before something starts hunting you.
- **desktop-only notice.** phones/tablets get a "⚠ DESKTOP ONLY" message instead of
  a dead ENTER button (the game needs a keyboard + mouse).
- **gameplay video** linked at the top of the README.

## the game so far

- **Backrooms Level 0** rebuilt from scratch in **Three.js / WebGL** — no engine,
  just HTML, CSS and JavaScript. rooms stream in endlessly around you.
- **M.E.G. trivia terminals** — 35 questions of real community Backrooms lore. read
  them (`E`) to hold your sanity together; log 5 correct answers and an exit door
  manifests.
- **sanity + blood** meters. the fluorescent hum drains sanity slowly; entities drain
  it fast. run out and the Backrooms keep you.
- **a monster** (Bacteria) that hunts you through the dark, and a glowing exit to
  escape through.
- **weapons** — fists, M16, shotgun, pistol, flashlight — crosshair combat.
- horror-font menus, in-game music, and the whole thing playable in a browser.

made by **Isabella Wu**. watch the lights.
